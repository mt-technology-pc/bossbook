SALES — INVOICES & SALES RECEIPTS

Two kinds of sale, both created from the Sales page:

INVOICE — a credit sale. Nothing is deposited immediately; it adds to the
customer's outstanding balance, due on the due date (defaults to 30 days
out). Reference codes look like S1, S2, S3...

SALES RECEIPT — a cash/bank sale paid on the spot. Money is deposited
immediately into a chosen cash/bank account, no balance is created.
Reference codes look like R1, R2, R3...

Both support:
- Multiple line items, with a searchable product picker that auto-fills
  the item's price.
- For products that track serial/IMEI numbers, a picker to choose exactly
  which physical units are being sold, itself searchable by serial number
  so a shop with many units in stock doesn't have to scroll a long list.
- An optional "Sales rep" field, attributing the sale to whoever made it
  (see the Sales Reps document).

Editing and deleting: sales can be edited or deleted from the Sales list
(pencil/trash icons on each row). Editing or deleting reverses and
reapplies the stock and account effects atomically — it is not just a
label change, it correctly adjusts inventory, serial unit status, and
account balances.
