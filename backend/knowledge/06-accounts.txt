ACCOUNTS (CASH/BANK) & EXPENSES

Real cash/bank accounts with a running balance. Tapping into an account
shows its full transaction ledger — every deposit and withdrawal that
built up to the current balance, sourced from actual sales, payments,
purchases paid, and expenses. Accounts get auto-numbered codes (ACC1,
ACC2...).

Expenses are recorded against a category and a specific cash/bank account
(which it withdraws from). Auto-numbered codes (EXP1, EXP2...).
