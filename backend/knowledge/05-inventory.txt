INVENTORY & SERIAL/IMEI TRACKING

The product catalog holds each product's name, price, cost, stock
quantity, and a "tracks serial/IMEI" toggle. Products get auto-numbered
codes (P1, P2, P3...).

When "tracks serial/IMEI" is on for a product, every unit purchased and
sold is tracked individually by its serial/IMEI number, not just as a
quantity:
- Buying stock: the purchase form asks for each unit's serial/IMEI number.
- Selling stock: the sale form shows a searchable picker of exactly which
  in-stock units are being sold.
- A dedicated "Serial Tracking" page (sidebar) lists every unit ever
  tracked, its current status (in stock / sold), which bill brought it
  in, and which sale (and customer) took it out — searchable by serial
  number, product, customer, or supplier, with CSV export. This is
  read-only reporting; there is currently no way to manually mark a unit
  as returned, damaged, or lost — status is only ever set automatically
  by recording a purchase or a sale.

Inventory Valuation (a report, see the Reports document) computes real
stock value using FIFO, Weighted Average, or Standard Cost — the user's
choice, not a fixed assumption.
