RECEIVE PAYMENT & PAY BILL

RECEIVE PAYMENT — records money collected from a customer, deposited to a
chosen account. Can optionally be linked to one specific outstanding
invoice so that invoice's balance reflects it, or left as a general
payment against the customer's overall balance. Has its own list page
(Payments Received) with edit/delete. Reference codes look like RCPT1,
RCPT2...

PAY BILL — the same idea for money paid out to a supplier against a
purchase bill. Has its own list page (Payments Made) with edit/delete.
Reference codes look like PAY1, PAY2...

Both are separate, dedicated pages — not just modals buried inside a
customer/supplier's detail page — so they support full search, edit, and
delete like every other record type.
