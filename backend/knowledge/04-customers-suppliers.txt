CUSTOMERS, SUPPLIERS, RECEIVABLES & PAYABLES

Customers and Suppliers each have a list page and a detail page showing
their full transaction history and a real, computed balance — not a
stored/cached number. The balance is always derived live from actual
invoices and payments, so it can never drift out of sync with reality.

Receivables and Payables are two dedicated sidebar pages (not buried
inside Reports) that list:
- Receivables: every customer who currently owes money, sorted by amount,
  with a one-click shortcut straight into Receive Payment with that
  customer already selected.
- Payables: every supplier you currently owe, sorted by amount, with the
  same one-click shortcut into Pay Bill.
