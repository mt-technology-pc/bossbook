SALES REPS

A dedicated sidebar page for tracking who on the sales team made which
sale. Add a rep (name, phone, email, notes — auto-numbered REP1, REP2...),
then optionally pick them on an Invoice or Sales Receipt at the point of
entry.

The Sales Reps list shows each rep's live total sales and sale count,
filterable by period (Today / This week / This month / a custom date
range / All time). Clicking into a rep shows their individual sales, plus
a breakdown grouped by day, week, or month.
