PURCHASES — SUPPLIER BILLS

Recording a bill from a supplier increases stock. Reference codes look
like B1, B2, B3...

Supports multiple line items and, for serial/IMEI-tracked products, entry
of each individual unit's serial number at the time of purchase.

Editing and deleting: bills can be edited or deleted the same way sales
can, with safety checks — you can't edit or delete a bill if any serial
unit it added has already been sold, or if doing so would drop stock
below zero. This prevents a bill edit from silently breaking a sale that
already happened.
