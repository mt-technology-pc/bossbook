RECORD NUMBERING

Every record type gets its own sequential, human-readable code that never
repeats, even after a restart:
- Customers: C1, C2...
- Products: P1, P2...
- Purchases (bills): B1, B2...
- Invoices: S1, S2...
- Sales receipts: R1, R2...
- Customer payments (Receive Payment): RCPT1, RCPT2...
- Supplier payments (Pay Bill): PAY1, PAY2...
- Expenses: EXP1, EXP2...
- Accounts (cash/bank): ACC1, ACC2...
- Sales reps: REP1, REP2...
