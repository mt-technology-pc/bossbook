BOSSBOOKS — OVERVIEW

BossBooks is an accounting and inventory system for small businesses. It is
not limited to mobile phone shops, though it has first-class support for
serial/IMEI-level stock tracking, which most general-purpose accounting
software lacks — useful for any business that sells individually
identifiable units (phones, laptops, appliances, equipment).

Currency: Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR) only right now, shown as "Rs. X". There
is no multi-currency support yet.

Theme: light only. There is no dark mode toggle.

Every number shown anywhere in the app — balances, stock levels, totals,
reports — is computed live from real recorded transactions. Nothing is a
placeholder or a guess.

Auth: standard email/password sign-in.
